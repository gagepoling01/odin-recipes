Honey Sriacha Chicken Tenders

Description

These honey sriracha chicken tenders are super easy to make and sure to hit the mark on sweet and spicy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken tenders (or breasts cut into strips)

Brine:

  • 1-2 cups Buttermilk
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tsp ground mustard
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of your favorite hot sauce
  • 1/4 cup of pickle juice

Breading:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of potato starch
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tsp ground mustard
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of black pepper

Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 1/4 cup of sriracha
  • 1 tsp soy sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare the brine in a bowl by mixing all the ingredients together.
  2. Put the chicken in the mixture and let marinate in the fridge for at least 1 hour (preferably overnight).
  3. Prepare the breading in a bowl by mixing all the ingredients together.
  4. Coat the brine-covered chicken tenders in the breading. Let rest for about 15 minutes on a wire rack to allow the flour to stick.
  5. Prepare the sauce by mixing all the ingredients in a bowl.
  6. Fry the chicken in oil at 300-325 degrees Fahrenheit for 5-6 minutes or until lightly golden.
  7. Fry again at 375-400 degrees Fahrenheit for 30 seconds to 1 minute, or until a deep golden brown.
  8. Coat the chicken in the prepared honey sriracha sauce and serve.
